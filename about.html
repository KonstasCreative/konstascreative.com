[ Who We Are ]

We Make Websites With Personality & Purpose

At Konstas Creative Web Design & Development, we build hand-crafted websites that work as hard as you do. Every line of code is written by hand—no templates, no shortcuts—so your site loads fast, performs flawlessly, and ranks well in Google. [ About Me ]

I’m Giorgos Konstas

I am the founder and lead developer of Konstas Creative Web Design & Development. When our first daughter arrived, I realized I wanted a career that let me be there for every milestone—so I traded my previous role for two years of late-night coding marathons. I taught myself how to code, built real sites from scratch, and immersed myself in front-end best practices.

Along the way, I talked with dozens of small-business owners and saw a familiar problem: either they couldn’t afford a quality website, or they paid upfront only to end up with a clunky, outdated design they didn’t trust. That’s why I launched this company with a simple promise:

  • Transparent, affordable pricing.
  • No hidden fees or surprise invoices.
  • Hand-crafted code, every time—fast, secure, mobile-first sites that earn you Google’s trust.

Let’s build something great together. Every pixel counts.

[ Our Work Process ]

We Take Care of Everything

Once you sign the contract and pay a €100 deposit, we’ll create initial design screens for your approval. After you sign off, we handle the full design and development, launch your site, and then support you for one month—covering any bugs or minor content tweaks.

1. Schedule a call.

We’ll chat about your goals and vision.

2. Create a custom proposal.

We craft key page mockups so you can visualize—and approve—the look.

3. Design & Develop

Upon design approval, we create your site and refine it with your feedback.

4. Launch & Support

We go live, then stay on hand for 30 days to fix bugs and make adjustments.
